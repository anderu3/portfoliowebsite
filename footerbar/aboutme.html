The YearUp Program

YearUp is a program that helps adults and young adults alike gain the skills they need to start successful careers. It offers a one-year training program that includes six months of rigorous learning and development and six months of an internship at a top company. During the L&D phase, students learn technical and professional skills in areas like IT, finance, and customer service, along with customary corporate etiquette and norms. In the internship phase, they apply what they've learned in a real-world setting, gaining valuable work experience. The goal of YearUp is to close the “Opportunity Divide,” which means helping young people from low-income backgrounds get access to the same job opportunities as those from more affluent backgrounds. YearUp partners with over 250 companies, providing a pathway to good jobs and higher education for its graduates.

If you'd like to dig deeper about YearUp's core values and their mission statements, please feel free to take a look at their About Page and let me know if you have any questions!
